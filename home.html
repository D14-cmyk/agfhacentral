About Our Church

The Assemblies Of God Church F.H.A. Central is a dynamic Pentecostal fellowship committed to reaching Nigeria and the world with the message of Jesus Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit. It was founded in the year 2011 FHA Lugbe, Abuja.

Church Departments

Men's Ministry

Building men of integrity and faith through fellowship, discipleship, and service.

Women's Ministry

Empowering women to grow spiritually and physically, build relationships, and serve the community.

Youth Ministry

Engaging and equipping the next generation to live out their faith in Christ.
